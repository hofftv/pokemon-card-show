# 🎒 The 5-Year-Old's Ultimate Pokémon Card Show Cheat Sheet

## 📖 The Rarity Dictionary
* **Holo (Holographic):** The picture of the Pokémon is shiny, but the rest of the card is normal. *(Cheap: $0.25 - $1.00)*
* **Reverse Holo:** The exact opposite! The entire card is shiny *except* for the picture of the Pokémon. *(Cheap: $0.25 - $1.00)*
* **V / ex / VSTAR / VMAX:** The modern standard for "Ultra Rares." The card is completely shiny, usually has silver borders. *(Affordable: $1.00 - $5.00)*
* **Full Art:** The Pokémon's artwork takes up the entire card from top to bottom. *(Mid-range: $3.00 - $25.00)*
* **IR (Illustration Rare) / TG (Trainer Gallery):** Takes up the whole card, but features a beautiful, highly detailed background or scene (like a watercolor painting). *(Mid-range: $3.00 - $30.00)*
