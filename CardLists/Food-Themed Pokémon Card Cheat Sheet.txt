🍦 The Food-Themed Pokémon Card Cheat Sheet
📖 The Rarity Dictionary (What do these words mean?)
When vendors throw around jargon, here is exactly what they mean:
* Holo (Holographic): The picture of the Pokémon is shiny, but the rest of the card is normal. (Cheap: $0.25 - $1.00)
* Reverse Holo: The exact opposite! The entire card is shiny except for the picture of the Pokémon. (Cheap: $0.25 - $1.00)
* V / ex / VSTAR / VMAX: The modern standard for "Ultra Rares." The card is completely shiny, usually has silver borders, and the Pokémon pops out of the picture box a little bit. (Affordable: $1.00 - $5.00)
* Full Art: The Pokémon's artwork takes up the entire card from top to bottom, but the background is usually a solid, textured color with no scenery. (Mid-range: $3.00 - $25.00)
* IR (Illustration Rare) / TG (Trainer Gallery): Takes up the whole card, but features a beautiful, highly detailed background or scene (like a watercolor painting or the Pokémon hanging out with its trainer). Highly sought after for the art. (Mid-range: $3.00 - $30.00)
📦 Sealed Product Guide
Always ask to pay cash to avoid taxes/fees. Do not buy "Evolving Skies," "151", or "Paldean Fates" as they carry a massive premium.
* Booster Packs (Single): Target $3.50 to $4.00 each.
* Booster Bundles (6 Packs): Target $20.00 to $23.00. (Best value for simply opening packs!)
* Elite Trainer Boxes (ETBs): Target $35.00 to $40.00. (Look for standard sets like Surging Sparks, Temporal Forces, or Paradox Rift).
